The Sport Rehab

Welcome to the Sport Rehab

Specializing in a functional and comprehensive approach to diagnose, treat & rehabilitate musculoskeletal impairments

My approach is aimed at identifying and treating the root cause of the pain and in turn relieving symptoms and preventing further injury. Muscles and Joints form a complex interconnected web. Tightness or weakness in any area of the body can affect the way we move and perform.

If left untreated these factors can lead to further problems elsewhere in the body.

Our services

The key to correction is first and foremost a professional diagnosis, followed by treatment and rehabilitation, a combination of specialist therapies. Our aim is to enable clients to be pain free and to reach their maximum potential in terms of movement and flexibility.

Rehabilitation Treatment

During your initial appointment we will take a medical history and examine you to assess and evaluate your injury. Once we have a diagnosis, we will create a tailor-made treatment program to addresses the root cause of the pain. We will start the treatment straight away.

Each client we see will have a unique injury or issue, treatment will be tailored to their needs. This will usually involve a combination of the therapies. We specialize in soft tissue massage, spinal and peripheral mobilisations, dry needling, taping, exercise programs and postural re-education.

Pilates

Available in 1:1 or 2:1 classes

Modified Pilates is an excellent form of exercises for clients with musculoskeletal problems, poor posture and sports injuries. Used in combination with other therapies, modified Pilates can help to strengthen any weak areas in the body, reducing the chance of reoccurring a recurrence of symptoms.
